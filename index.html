Why Epicodus?

As mentioned in my "About Me" section, my interest in coding was sparked by of course, MySpace.

While my professional background ranges from caretaking, to warehouse management, the joy brought to me by coding never went away. I simply was not sure how to get my foot in the door in the tech industry.
This is why I was so glad to learn about Epicodus.

I have always loved learning new things, building things, and solving problems. Coding is a career that will genuinely always have all three of those things, which makes it perfect for me.

I am thankful that Epicodus has a part time option to fit my schedule, and also offers help finding work/internships after the bootcamp. I appreciate that the course not only teaches you how to code, but also good practices on learning code, and how to create professional, well-refactored code.

I am also extremely grateful that Epicodus puts such a focus on Diversity, Equity and Inclusion! The tech industry does seem daunting when you are a person living in a marginalized body, so it is comforting to see systemic issues actively discussed. I also always love learning and being educated on best social practices. I do my best to always be as polite and supportive as possible, and I appreciate that the courses at Epicodus can help me foster growth in that area of my life.
